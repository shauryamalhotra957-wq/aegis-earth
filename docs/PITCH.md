# Aegis Earth Pitch

## One-Liner

Aegis Earth is an explainable AI command center that helps emergency leaders decide what to do first when climate, infrastructure, and public-health crises collide.

## The Problem

Disaster response is drowning in data and starving for clear decisions. Satellites, weather offices, hospitals, responders, shelters, roads, and social reports all generate signals, but leaders still need a transparent answer to three urgent questions:

1. Where is risk highest?
2. What action protects the most people fastest?
3. Is the plan fair to vulnerable communities?

## The Product

Aegis Earth combines:

- Multi-hazard risk scoring.
- Signal trust scoring.
- Constrained resource optimization.
- Equity guardrails.
- 72-hour outcome simulation.
- Exportable executive brief.

## Why It Stands Out

Most dashboards show what is happening. Aegis Earth argues for what to do next and explains why.

The demo is deliberately self-contained, tested, and safe: synthetic data by default, optional public weather alerts, no secrets, no hidden backend, and no claim that it replaces official emergency systems.

## Demo Flow

1. Open the command center and point to the five global metrics.
2. Select `Ganges Delta` in the triage list and show why it ranks first.
3. Click `Shock` in Scenario Lab and show the 72-hour projection changing.
4. Select `Los Angeles Basin` and show the plan adapts to wildfire and infrastructure risk.
5. Click the download icon in the intel panel to export the executive brief.
6. Close with the tests, build, audit, and architecture docs.

## Closing Line

Aegis Earth is not another map. It is a decision engine for the moments when delays cost lives.
